Budget Travel Guides

Explore Ghana and Africa without breaking the bank.

Top Budget-Friendly Destinations

  • Wli Waterfalls – Volta Region
  • Shai Hills Resource Reserve – Greater Accra
  • Nzulezo Village on Stilts – Western Region
  • Kakum National Park – Central Region

Money-Saving Travel Tips

  • Travel during off-peak seasons
  • Book local guesthouses early
  • Eat local food for cheaper and healthier options
  • Use public transport or ride-sharing apps

Free Activities to Enjoy

  • City walking tours
  • Beach hopping along the coast
  • Visiting local markets
  • Community cultural festivals (ask locals!)
